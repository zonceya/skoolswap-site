Community Guidelines

Last updated:

We want SkoolSwap to be a safe and trusted marketplace for school communities.

1. Be Honest

Describe items truthfully; Show real photos; Do not hide damage.

2. Be Respectful

No harassment; No bullying; No profanity; Treat school community members respectfully.

3. Safe Meet-Ups

Meet at school or safe public places; Confirm identity before handing items; Never exchange items without app confirmation.

4. Allowed Items

School uniforms; Books; Bags; Sports gear; Approved school accessories.

5. Not Allowed

Weapons; Electronics without proof of ownership; Broken or unusable items; Fake uniforms; Stolen goods.

6. Fraud Prevention

Use escrow only; Never accept cash; Never release items without the PIN; Report suspicious activity immediately.
