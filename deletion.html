Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated:

If you used Facebook Login or Google Sign-In, you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Email admin@skoolswap.co.za with subject “Delete My Account”
  2. Include the email you used to sign in
  3. Your data will be removed within 7 days

What Will Be Deleted

  • Profile
  • Chat messages
  • Marketplace listings
  • Escrow/payout history (except required financial records)
  • Login tokens

What Cannot Be Deleted Immediately

For legal and financial reasons, some transaction records must be stored for up to 5 years, as required by law.
