Why SkoolSwap?

SkoolSwap connects schools, parents, and learners. Sell and buy safely within your community with verified school shops, secure escrow payments, and a growing marketplace for uniforms, stationery, sports kits, and more.

Our Features

Verified School Shops

Official school shops marked as verified. Parents & learners can trust the source and school receives revenue from every transaction.

Free Selling

Schools, parents, and learners can sell their preloved items completely free. Buyers pay a 5% service fee.

Escrow Payments

Buyers pay into a secure escrow system. Funds are released to the seller once the item is confirmed delivered.

Delivery Coming Soon

Soon you'll be able to choose PAXI, PostNet, or local courier services.
